About Us

Empowering the Nation is a small-to-mid-size enterprise that strives to provide skills training for people with less opportunities like domestic workers and gardeners. The founder, Precious Radebe, was inspired to start this initiative after witnessing up-close how the struggles of not receiving formal educational qualifications can affect people and their families; this helped encourage her to provide the education necessary to her community. To assist with this endeavor, a website and an application has been decided is the best way to give people access to the courses the company offers.
